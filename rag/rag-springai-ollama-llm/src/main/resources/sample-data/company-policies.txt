# Company Bonus and Discount Policies

## End of Year Bonus Formula
The end of year bonus for all eligible employees is calculated as follows:
Bonus = (Base Salary * 0.10) + (Years of Service * 500)
If the employee is in the sales department, add an additional (Total Sales * 0.05).

## Bulk Discount Rules
When customers purchase units in bulk, the following discounts apply to the base unit price of $150:
- For 1 to 4 units: No discount.
- For 5 to 9 units: 10% discount on the total price.
- For 10 or more units: 20% discount on the total price.
The total cost is calculated as: (Number of Units * Base Unit Price) * (1 - Discount Percentage).

## Pricing Tiers for Software Subscription
- Basic Tier: $20 per user per month.
- Pro Tier: $45 per user per month.
- Enterprise Tier: $100 per user per month.
An annual subscription (12 months) receives a 15% discount on the total yearly cost.
